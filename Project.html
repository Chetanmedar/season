SEASONS

Monsoon Season

  • Months: June - July
  • Harvesting Time : September - October
  • Crops Grown :
    • Rice (Paddy)
    • Maize (Corn)
    • Bajra (Pearl Millet)
    • Jowar (Sorghum)
    • Soybean
    • Cotton
    • Groundnut
  • Seasonal Feature : Depends on rainfall.

Winter Season

  • Months: October - November
  • Harvesting Time : March - April
  • Crops Grown :
    • Wheat
    • Barley
    • Mustard
    • Peas
    • Chana
    • Oats
  • Seasonal Feature : Grows in cold season, harvested in spring.

Summer Season

  • Months: March - April
  • Harvesting Time : June (before monsoon)
  • Crops Grown :
    • Watermelon
    • Muskmelon
    • Cucumber
    • Vegetables (Pumpkin, Bitter ground, etc.)
    • Fodder Crops
  • Seasonal Feature : Short duration crops, grown between Monsoon and Winter.
